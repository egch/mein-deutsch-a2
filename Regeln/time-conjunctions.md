# Time Conjunctions

---

## als — when (single event in the past)

Use **als** for a specific moment or period in the past (happens once).

> *Als ich jung war...* — When I was young...
> *Als ich ankam, war er weg.* — When I arrived, he was gone.

---

## wenn — when / whenever (present, future, repeated past)

Use **wenn** for present, future, or repeated events in the past.

> *Wenn ich Zeit habe, lese ich.* — When I have time, I read.
> *Wenn ich jung war, spielte ich Fußball.* — Whenever I was young, I played football. *(repeated)*

---

## Quick Rule

| Situation                        | Use   |
|----------------------------------|-------|
| Single event in the past         | als   |
| Present / future                 | wenn  |
| Repeated events (past or present)| wenn  |
